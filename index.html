Who I Am

Hi, call me mig. I grew up fixing computers while living in Long Beach. After several years of searching for my purpose in life, I found a passion for programming. I'm now living and working in Orange County.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me hiking, exploring a new city, at a brewery, gaming, biking or skating.

Where I Work

I'm currently working with amazing people at the video game company NCSOFT. I've been fortunate enough to work with many different technologies since I was able to work on every project the web team has built. The work I do include building web applications, internal tools, npm packages, reusable react components, and bug fixing.


I see coding as an adventure.
