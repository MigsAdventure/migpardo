Who I Am

Hi, call me mig. Based off Long Beach CA, I grew up fixing computers. After several years of searching for my purpose in life, I found a passion for programming.

I've built several websites and web apps. I'm now working on bigger projects and increasing my skillset each day.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me hiking, exploring a new city, biking or skating.

I've always loved to piece things together. Every line of code I write is a new piece of a puzzle. There's nothing more satisfying than seeing all of the pieces come together.


I see coding as an adventure.
